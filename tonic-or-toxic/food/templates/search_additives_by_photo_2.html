Step 2 - Upload a photo

The quality of your image will have a crucial impact on the result! When capturing a photo, follow the tips provided below.

  • Make sure to capture the complete list of food ingredients
  • For optimal horizontal text capture, utilize grid lines
  • Follow device suggestions
  • Refrain from using flash
  • Trim the image to remove any unnecessary elements
  • If your device allows, aim for a photo resolution of approximately 200 pixels per inch (ppi)
